Fractured Night

The story has two sides. Can you discover the truth?

Public Story

The Mayor

The mayor performed maintenance checks on the bridge and managed the city budget responsibly.

He publicly blames the engineer and supervisor to maintain trust in the city.

The public praises his actions; the engineer and supervisor are arrested.

True Story

The Mayor

In reality, the mayor restricted maintenance and redirected budget for personal gain and reputation management.

The engineer was actually trying to fix the bridge, and the supervisor ensured safety with limited resources.

The public was misled, and the mayor’s manipulation went unnoticed.
